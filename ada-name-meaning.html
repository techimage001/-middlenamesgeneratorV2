Ada: meaning, origin and middle names

Where Ada comes from, what it carries, and which middle names its sound actually invites.

Ada comes from Germanic, from a root meaning noble: carried by Ada Lovelace, who wrote the first computer algorithm.

How Ada sounds in a full name

Ada runs to two beats. It closes on an open “ah”, which means vowel-opening middle names slur into it out loud. At two beats, Ada sits comfortably with one- and three-syllable middles; a two-syllable middle gives a steadier 2–2 rhythm, which some ears prefer and others find flat.
