Questions parents ask

What middle names mean hope?

Hope, Nadia, Esperanza, Amal all carry the meaning, across different languages: Hope is English for the virtue itself; Nadia is Slavic for hope; Esperanza is Spanish for hope.

Does a name's meaning matter more than its sound?

Meaning is why a name reaches your shortlist; sound is why it stays there. A name that means exactly what you want but slurs into your surname will be misheard for eighty years. Test the whole name aloud before the meaning decides it for you.
