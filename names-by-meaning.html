Why meaning-first works best for middle names

First names carry the daily weight of fashion and playgrounds; the middle spot is where meaning gets to lead. A middle name chosen for its sense: Asher for happiness, Noor for light, Boaz for strength: gives the full name a quiet story that outlasts every trend. Each result above shows its origin, and where scholars genuinely disagree about an etymology, the entry says "debated" rather than inventing certainty; that honesty policy is explained on the meanings lookup.
