Questions parents ask

What makes a good greek middle name?

Meaning gets you onto the shortlist; sound gets you onto the birth certificate. Choose two or three from the lists above, then test each one against your actual first name and surname: the join between the names is where most beautiful choices quietly fail.

Do the origins of the first and middle name have to match?

No. Mixed-origin names are extremely common and almost never remarked on. How the two names sound together matters far more than where they came from.
