Last updated: 9 July 2026

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. This page lists what this site uses and how to control it. The names you type into our tools are never stored in cookies or anywhere else.

Advertising cookies (consent-based in UK/EEA)

We show ads through Google AdSense to keep the tools free. Google and certified ad partners may set cookies (such as those on the doubleclick.net and google.com domains) to serve ads, cap how often you see the same ad, measure performance, and — only where you consent — personalise ads based on your visits to this and other sites. If you decline consent, ads shown are non-personalised and rely only on context (like the page topic).

Details of Google's advertising cookies: policies.google.com/technologies/cookies.

Managing cookies

  • Change your consent here at any time by clearing this site's cookies in your browser — the consent banner will reappear on your next visit.
  • Control personalised ads at Google Ad Centre, aboutads.info or youronlinechoices.eu.
  • Block or delete cookies entirely through your browser settings (Settings → Privacy in Chrome, Safari, Firefox and Edge). The tools on this site work fully with all cookies blocked.
